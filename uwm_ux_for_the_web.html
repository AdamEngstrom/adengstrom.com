UWM & UX for the Web

The Problem

During our class, we had to come up with a business idea, complete user interviews, create a persona and wireframes of a website/app, and perform a usability test.

The Users

Our users were our fellow classmates but were meant to be symbolic of the general population.

The Process

The business plan my partner and I came up with was an app that would streamline people's experiences with doctors and making appointments.

Using interviews of our other classmates, we developed a persona for our average user (shown below). Our interviews showed that there was a need for a more user friendly appointment making process.

The Results

The following images I took quickly with my phone are a few of the hand-drawn wireframes my partner and I created for our app entitled MobileDoc. The app was designed to give users greater control over their healthcare by streamlining making appointments, choosing the right doctors, and keeping track of information.
